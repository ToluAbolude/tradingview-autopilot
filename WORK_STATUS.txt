  1. TRUE 90%+ WR is achieved via multi-TP + breakeven architecture:
     - Enter at high-confluence level (score ≥ 10)

     - TP2 at 1.5R (take another 40% of position)

     - Change of Character (CHoCH): first BOS in opposite direction of trend

     - Retest of broken level: enter at the retest (support → resistance flip)
     - Confirmation: rejection candle (pin bar / engulfing) at retest
     - WR: 65-80% with 1:2 R:R minimum
     - Best instruments: XAUUSD, GBPUSD, EURUSD, BTC (large moves, clean S/R)
     - Best timeframes: 15M entry, 1H/4H structure

     - NY futures open at 13:30 UTC

     - 14:30 UTC: US economic data releases (when no news: pure price action)
     - User note: 2:30-2:45 PM London time = 13:30-13:45 UTC = NY open peak
